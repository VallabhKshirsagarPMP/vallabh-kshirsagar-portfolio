About Me

I am a senior project and delivery leader currently working with Deloitte, supporting complex AI & Data and enterprise transformation initiatives. My work sits at the intersection of execution discipline, stakeholder alignment, and modern delivery practices.

I am known for bringing structure when things are ambiguous and for owning outcomes when delivery matters most.
